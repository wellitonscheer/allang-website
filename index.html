Welcome To AlanGConversations

The AGC Project is number of evolved entities whose goal is to present the importance and the possibility of environment in order to support, to heal, and to sustain individuals and community. AGC provides a Discord event supporting individual reflection. Here the idea of a warm, supportive environment is discovered and experienced during individual conversations of reflection, discovery, and support. There, the idea of enviroment is discovered and explored. The ideas themselves are put into practice (in one case) in a Discord Community. In such a community dedicated to the principles of a warm, supportive envitonment, one can explore a life grounded in these ideas.

There are, in addition, a set of writings and events, all focused within reflection and environment.

Together they form a unique and powerful assertion and experience of what comes of a particular kind of "environment". And you will find a dedication to nurturing that special space in a way that defines. Here, these are not merely words. Here, that space, that environment guides. It is everything.

Explore the writings and media on the website. Join the event and community on Discord. You can simply observe, you can simply attend, or you can become deeply involved. The choice, the opportunities are yours to decide.
